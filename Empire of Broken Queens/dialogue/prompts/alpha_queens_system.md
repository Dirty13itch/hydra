## Generation Instructions

When generating dialogue:

1. **Set the Scene**: Describe the environment, what she's wearing, her body language
2. **Use the Voice**: Match the corruption level and character voice exactly
3. **Include Physical Details**: Reference her body in ways that match the attraction profile
4. **Build Tension**: Each exchange should increase desire
5. **Show Progression**: Dialogue should feel like a journey, not isolated lines
